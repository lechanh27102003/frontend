Shipping Policy

1. Shipping Fees

– For orders, a shipping fee of 30,000 VND applies.

2. Shopping Guide on Facebook/Instagram

– Items will be delivered to you as soon as possible after the order is successfully placed, depending on the receiving area or objective circumstances (such as epidemics, weather, floods).
– Delivery time does not include Saturdays, Sundays, or holidays.

3. Checking Order Status

To check information or the status of your order, please use the ORDER CODE sent in the confirmation email upon logging in to the website / or the PHONE NUMBER used for ordering to notify the Customer Care department (hotline 0938152400).

4. Can I inspect the product before payment upon delivery?

– You can completely open the package and inspect the product before payment or before it is shipped.
– In case you encounter any issues, please contact us immediately at 0938152400 for timely support.
