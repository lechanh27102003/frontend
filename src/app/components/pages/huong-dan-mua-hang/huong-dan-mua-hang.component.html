Shopping Guide

1. Plantique's Operating Channels

Currently, Plantique operates primarily on the following channels:
Facebook: Plantique
Instagram: Plantique
Website: https://plantique.vn/

2. How to Shop on Facebook/Instagram

– Search and select the product you like and wish to purchase.
– Send a direct message to Plantique's Facebook inbox. Our staff will assist you with product information (price, current product status, etc.).
– Finally, we will confirm the order details and proceed with packaging and shipping.

3. How to Shop on Website

STEP 1: Search and Select Products on the Website using one of the following methods:
– Enter the product name in the search bar.
– Browse product categories using the main menu.
Each product on the WEBSITE has complete product information and detailed images.
Here, you can choose the quantity of the product you want to purchase by clicking the ADD TO CART button.
STEP 2: Proceed to Place Order and Payment
STEP 3: Order Confirmation
– After completing the online shopping process on the Plantique website, you will receive a confirmation via email.

4. How to Shop on Shopee, Lazada

Visit the Shopee or Lazada app, within Plantique's store, to select and purchase your favorite items!

5. How to Shop on Tiktok Shop

Visit Plantique Shop's Tiktok channel, go to the shopping cart icon to select your favorite items and proceed to pay for your order directly within the app.
